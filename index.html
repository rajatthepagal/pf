MEGRID-a-CSS-Responsive-Framework

I am not a BIG FAN of bootstrap. Ok i love its functionality and i understand its purpose of giving quick results when prototyping. But i have seen my friends and fellow learners to use it everytime whenever they want grids. Its like getting a hammer for killing a fly. So i though why not write my own Framework for this purpose. So i present MEGRID. As straight as the documentation says it gives you a grid and a slide menu bar in mobile view. Its is fully cutomizable since CSS specificity is to its minimum in the source code. And ya also this portfolio is build using it.

Quick Test - an Online Test Portal

This one project was made in collaboration with two of my batchmates Amrit Jha and Meghali Nandi. The Quick Test is a portal for anyone to conduct a test. It was intended for closed LAN application in college campuses. The teacher can log in as an Admin in it and hoist a test on a prticular date. The Student can then log in his/her account to check for the test and appear in them. They get immediate report and teacher gets an immediate report of prformance.
STACK: JavaEE , Servlets , JSP ,JSTL , Apache Tomcat, SQL , JDBC
Front End(behaves as Single page App): JQuerry , CSS3 , HTML5
